mashed potatoes

mashed potatoes are a really good option if you dint have more then 5 mins on your hand you will need

  • potatoes
  • 1/3 milk or sour cream
thats it! to prepare it follow the following steps
  1. boil the potatoes for 10mins untill they are really soft
  2. mash them using any spoon with a little bigger surface area
  3. add bit milk or sour cream and season it with salt and pepper
